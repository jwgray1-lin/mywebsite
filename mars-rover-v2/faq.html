What is a Mars rover?

A Mars rover is a robotic vehicle designed to move across the Martian surface and carry scientific instruments to study rocks, soil, atmosphere, and signs of past habitability.

What does NASA do?

NASA leads U.S. civil space exploration and aeronautics research, including missions like the Mars rovers, space telescopes, and human spaceflight.

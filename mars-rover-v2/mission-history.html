Mission History

An overview of major milestones from Sojourner through Perseverance.

Timeline

Sojourner (Mars Pathfinder)

1997

First successful Mars rover, validating mobile surface exploration.

Spirit & Opportunity

2004

Twin MER rovers far exceeded their 90-sol mission plans and found extensive evidence of past water processes.

Curiosity (MSL)

2012

Mobile laboratory analyzing Mars' geology and climate in Gale Crater to assess past habitability.

Perseverance & Ingenuity

2021

Sample caching in Jezero Crater and technology demonstration of powered flight with Ingenuity.
